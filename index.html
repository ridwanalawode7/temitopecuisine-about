Temitope Cuisine
Temitope Cuisine is a Nigerian based bakery created on March 2020.It has a bakery shop located opposite Summit University Offa, Kwara State. Its delicious recipies and fast delivey service has made it one of the leading bakeries in the country. It is currently making delivery service to all 36 states of the country and the Federal Capital Teritory. And has plans to expand to to at least 10 countries before the end of 2020. Temitope Cuisine has a wide range of recipies from cakes to popular Nigeria snacks like, chin chin, doughnut, popcorn and many more.
The Owner
Bamidele Khadijah Kehinde Temitope is the founder, manager and chief chef of Temitope Cuisine. She is a native of Offa and a student of the Obafemi Awolowo University(OAU). Having recieved training in caterine services she decided to start her own bakery offering one of the best recipies the world has to offer.
